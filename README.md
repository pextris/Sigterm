# SIGTERM

> They built it. We broke in. Now we live here.

A free, open source, browser-based multiplayer RPG inspired by BBS door games of the 1980s. No install required to play. Real players. Play for 10 minutes or lose an entire evening.

## How It Works

- **The Grid** - 10 turns daily. Fight corp programs. Earn credits. Level up. Come back tomorrow.
- **Daily Missions** - Three missions every day. Complete them for bonus credits and XP. Reset at midnight.
- **OffOps** - Multi-floor strikes against corp infrastructure. Die and lose everything. Clear the boss and keep it all.
- **Stats** - Kills. Credits. Level. Streak. PvP record. Achievements. Every runner has a profile. Every profile tells a story.
- **The Leaderboard** - One global leaderboard. Real players. Real competition. Your score is your legacy.
- **The Public Vault** - Fills all season from player activity. Pays out when the season ends. Everyone can see it.
- **Factions** - Join Deadlock, Cipher Syndicate, or Ghost Protocol. Faction wars happen. Sides matter.
- **The Council** - Top 10 runners vote weekly on what happens next in the story. Real consequences.
- **The Season** - Ends when a runner defeats the Megacorp AI. The runner who ends it is written into the Archive permanently.
- **Achievements** - 36 achievements with genuine humor and real rewards. Some are secret. Some are very secret.
- **The Archive** - A living lore document that unlocks as you progress. A signal in the Dead Drop that some runners have noticed and most haven't.
- **Mentor System** - High level runners take on students. Skills that cannot be learned any other way. Lineages that pass down through seasons.
- **Obituaries** - When a runner goes dark for 30 days Static broadcasts something. Not sad. Just they were here. They mattered.
- **Private Messages** - Leave a note on someone's profile that only they can see. No notifications. Just a message waiting for someone when they get back.

## The Sysop System

Every node has a Sysop. One voice the grid amplifies above all others.

On the mothership that is pextris. The one who built it. Still here. Still running.

When you self-host your own node you become the Sysop of your grid. You choose up to three Trusted runners who carry your authority. You set the tone. You write the transmissions. You build the culture.

The Sysop's messages appear in the Dead Drop in a color nobody else gets. Lyra knows their order before they sit down. Static plays their song on login without being asked.

Every node is its own small world. Every Sysop is its own Seth Able. The grid is a federation. Each node has its own mythology. All of them share the same Archive, the same season, the same signal transmitting somewhere in the deep grid.

This is what BBS culture was. This is what it can be again.

## Quick Install

    git clone https://github.com/pextris/Sigterm.git
    cd Sigterm
    bash install.sh

The installer asks your node name, your Sysop handle, your Trusted runners, port, Anthropic API key, and admin password. Your node is live. Your mythology begins.

## The Stack

- Backend: Node.js, Express
- Database: SQLite via better-sqlite3
- Frontend: React (Vite)
- Process manager: PM2
- Auth: JWT
