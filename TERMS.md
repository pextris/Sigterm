# SIGTERM — Terms of Service

Simple version first, detailed version below.

**Simple version:** The game is free. Your data stays on the node you play on. We don't sell anything. The grid holds space.

---

## The Game

SIGTERM is free to play. It always will be. No gameplay feature will ever be paywalled.

The game is MIT licensed and open source. You can run your own node. You can fork it. You can build on it.

---

## Your Data

Your account data lives on the node you registered on. It does not leave that node.

Your password is stored encrypted. It never leaves your node. The mothership cannot see it. Other nodes cannot see it. We cannot see it.

If you play on the mothership node at alpha.blipsandbloops.com your data is stored on our servers. We do not sell it. We do not share it. We use it only to run the game.

If you play on a community node your data is controlled by that node's Sysop. We have no control over community nodes. Choose your node accordingly.

---

## The Economy

GRT and credits are in-game currencies. They have no real world value. They cannot be purchased with real money. They cannot be sold or exchanged for real money or anything with real world value.

They are game mechanics. Nothing more.

---

## Supporter Tiers

Supporter tiers on Patreon are purchases of specific cosmetic items and services. They are not donations. They are not investments. They do not grant ownership, equity, decision-making power, or gameplay advantage.

What each tier gets is exactly what it says it gets. Nothing more.

---

## Community Nodes

Community nodes are operated independently by their Sysops. The mothership is not responsible for what happens on community nodes. Community node Sysops are responsible for their own communities and must comply with applicable laws in their jurisdiction.

Community node databases are separate from the mothership. The mothership shares aggregated data only. Never raw player data.

---

## Content

Don't be a landlord in real life either. Keep it clean. Keep it human. The grid holds space for everyone.

We reserve the right to remove content or accounts that harm the community.

## Changes

If these terms change we'll announce it in the Dead Drop. The grid is transparent.
